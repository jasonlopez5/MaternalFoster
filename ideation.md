1. Which audiences did you consider, and which one did you settle on for your app?
   * Audiences I considered were pregnant women, individuals with specific health goals, and soccer fans/players. I settled on pregnant women. 
2. List the ten needs that you identified for your chosen audience
   * Access to trustworthy pregnancy information
   * A way to track pregnancy progress week by week
   * Easy access to health and nutrition advice
   * Information about warning signs during pregnancy
   * Emotional support and encouragement during pregnancy
   * A due date calculator
   * Organization for appointments and reminders
   * Post-birth care information for both mother and baby
   * A simple and easy-to-use interface
   * Personalized resources based on pregnancy stage
3. State the three needs that were chosen out of the ten, and state the projects that you mapped to those needs
   * 1st need: Access to trustworthy pregnancy information
      * Project mapped: MaternalFoster educational pregnancy app
   * 2nd Need: Organization for appointments and reminders
      * Project mapped: Pregnancy planner and reminder system
   * 3rd Need: Pregnancy progress tracking
      * Project mapped: Week-by-week pregnancy tracker with due date calculator
5. For each of the three projects, list the goals/ideas that you brainstormed for them:
   * Pregnancy health information app
      * Provide reliable pregnancy information from trusted sources
      * Include nutrition and warning sign sections
      * Make information easy to understand
      * Create a calming and supportive design
   * Pregnancy planner and reminder app
      * Allow users to keep track of doctor appointments
      * Add reminders for medications and checkups
      * Help users stay organized during pregnancy
      * Include a simple calendar layout
   * Week-by-week pregnancy tracker app
      * Show pregnancy progress week by week
      * Include a due date calculator
      * Display trimester information and milestones
      * Give tips based on the current stage of pregnancy
7. State the “winner” of this process
   * The winner of this process is MaternalFoster.
